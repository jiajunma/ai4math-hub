## ICML 2025 AI4Math Highlights

### Dedicated Track: Mathematical Reasoning
First year with a dedicated track for mathematical reasoning research.

### Key Papers

**Neural Theorem Proving**
- Policy gradient methods for tactic prediction
- Transformer architectures for proof representation
- Graph neural networks for premise selection

**Problem Solving**
- Olympiad-level geometry with neural-symbolic methods
- Automated inequality proving
- Combinatorial search with neural guidance

**Training Methodologies**
- Curriculum learning for mathematical reasoning
- Synthetic data generation for math problems
- Reinforcement learning from human proofs

### Tutorials
- **Introduction to Neural Theorem Proving**: Lean and Coq
- **Mathematical Reasoning in LLMs**: From GPT-4 to specialized models

### Competitions
- **IMO-Grand Challenge**: Progress report and new benchmarks
- **miniF2F**: Neural theorem proving competition

## Impact
ICML 2025 marked mainstream acceptance of AI4Math as a core ML research area.
