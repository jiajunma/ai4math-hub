## Abstract

NuminaMath-CoT is an open-source language model specifically trained for mathematical reasoning using chain-of-thought (CoT) methodology. Released as part of the AI-MO NuminaMath competition.

## Training Data

- **860K+ samples**: Mathematical problems with CoT solutions
- **Diverse sources**: Competition math, textbooks, synthetic data
- **Quality filtering**: Multi-stage filtering for high-quality training

## Performance

- **MATH dataset**: Strong performance for open-source model
- **Competition math**: Good at AMC/AIME level problems
- **Chain-of-thought**: Explicit reasoning improves accuracy

## NuminaMath Competition

Part of the AI-MO initiative to advance mathematical AI:
- Open competition for mathematical problem solving
- Dataset released for research
- Community-driven improvement
