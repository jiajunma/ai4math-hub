## Study Overview

Frenzy Math conducted a comprehensive study of 5,000 students preparing for AMC 10/12 competitions over a 6-month period.

## Key Findings

- **Score Improvement**: 40% average increase in AMC practice test scores
- **Study Time**: Students used the platform 4+ hours per week
- **Retention**: 85% of students continued using the platform after 3 months
- **Engagement**: 90% reported increased confidence in problem-solving

## Methodology

- Pre/post test design
- Control group using traditional study methods
- Statistical significance: p < 0.001

## Implications

Demonstrates that AI-powered personalized tutoring can significantly improve mathematics competition performance at scale.
