## Abstract

Qwen2.5-Math is a series of specialized mathematical language models that achieve state-of-the-art performance on mathematical reasoning benchmarks, outperforming GPT-4o and Claude 3.5 Sonnet.

## Key Results

- **MATH**: 83.6% (7B model)
- **GSM8K**: 96.2%
- **OlympiadBench**: 41.6%

## Model Variants

- **Qwen2.5-Math-7B**: Best efficiency/performance ratio
- **Qwen2.5-Math-72B**: Highest absolute performance
- **Qwen2.5-Math-Instruct**: Instruction-tuned version

## Technical Innovations

- **Math-specific pre-training**: Extensive mathematical corpus
- **Process Reward Model**: Step-by-step reasoning training
- **Tool integration**: Calculator and Python interpreter support

## Open Source

Fully open-source model from Alibaba Cloud, demonstrating China's leadership in open AI research.
