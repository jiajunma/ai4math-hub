## AI4Math at NeurIPS 2025

### Workshops
- **MATH-AI**: Mathematical Reasoning in AI (3rd edition)
- **Formal Verification**: Neural methods for formal verification
- **AI for Science**: Mathematics and scientific discovery

### Notable Papers

**Theorem Proving**
- TheoremLLaMA: Transforming LLMs into Lean4 experts
- Expert Iteration 2.0: Improved self-play for proof search
- Neural-guided SAT solving for combinatorics

**Mathematical Reasoning**
- Multimodal math: Vision-language models for geometry
- Chain-of-thought verification: Reducing reasoning errors
- Tool-augmented LLMs for symbolic integration

**Autoformalization**
- Large-scale autoformalization of arXiv papers
- Natural language to Isabelle/HOL translation
- Bidirectional informal-formal alignment

### Awards
- **Best Paper**: Deep symbolic regression discovers physical laws
- **Outstanding Paper**: Neural theorem prover for undergraduate math
