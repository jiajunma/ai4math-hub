## CICM 2025 Overview

CICM (Conferences on Intelligent Computer Mathematics) is the premier venue for:
- Automated theorem proving
- Mathematical knowledge management
- Formal verification
- Digital mathematical libraries

### Tracks

**Calculemus**: Symbolic computation and reasoning
- Integration of SMT solvers with proof assistants
- Certified symbolic computation
- Automated reasoning in education

**Digital Mathematical Libraries**
- MathML and mathematical markup
- Search in mathematical corpora
- Formalization of mathematical archives

**Mathematical Knowledge Management**
- Ontologies for mathematics
- Collaborative formalization
- Version control for proofs

**Formal Methods**
- Verification of AI systems
- Formalized mathematics
- Proof engineering

### Keynotes
- "The Future of Formal Mathematics" - Kevin Buzzard
- "AI-Assisted Proof Engineering" - Leonardo de Moura

## Importance
CICM is the most focused venue for AI4Math research bridging formal methods and AI.
