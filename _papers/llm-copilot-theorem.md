## Abstract

We explore the use of large language models as interactive copilots for theorem proving. Our study shows that human-AI collaboration can significantly improve productivity in formal verification.

## Key Findings

- LLMs can suggest useful proof steps
- Human feedback improves model suggestions
- Significant productivity gains observed

## Applications

- Educational tools for learning theorem proving
- Professional formal verification workflows
