## Abstract

We introduce MMMU (Massive Multi-discipline Multimodal Understanding and Reasoning Benchmark), a new benchmark designed to evaluate multimodal AI on college-level multidisciplinary tasks requiring expert-level reasoning.

## Features

- **11.5K** questions across 6 disciplines
- **College-level** difficulty
- **Multimodal**: Images, diagrams, text
- **Math heavy**: Strong math component

## Impact

New standard benchmark for evaluating AI mathematical reasoning capabilities.
