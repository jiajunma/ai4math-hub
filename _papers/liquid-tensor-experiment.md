## Abstract

We report on the completion of the Liquid Tensor Experiment. This project, initiated by Peter Scholze, aimed to formalize a foundational result from condensed mathematics using the Lean proof assistant.

## Background

In December 2020, Peter Scholze posed a challenge to formalize a theorem from his work on condensed mathematics. He offered to personally review the formalization, marking a unique collaboration between a leading mathematician and the formalization community.

## The Theorem

The main theorem concerns the vanishing of certain Ext-groups in the category of condensed abelian groups. It is a foundational result for analytic geometry in the condensed setting.

## Timeline

- **Dec 2020**: Challenge issued by Peter Scholze
- **Jun 2021**: Core theorem formalized (6 months)
- **Jun 2022**: Complete proof verified

## Impact

Demonstrated that:
1. Research-level mathematics can be formalized
2. Collaborative formalization works at scale
3. Lean/mathlib is mature enough for serious math

## Significance

Major milestone in formal mathematics, attracting attention from mainstream mathematics community.
