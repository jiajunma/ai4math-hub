## Announcement

Frenzy Math announced the launch of their AMC 10/12 AI Tutor, a specialized system for competition mathematics preparation.

## Key Features

- **Personalized Learning**: Adaptive difficulty based on student level
- **AMC Problem Database**: Thousands of past AMC problems with solutions
- **Step-by-Step Hints**: Socratic questioning approach
- **Progress Tracking**: Detailed analytics on strengths and weaknesses
- **Mock Tests**: Simulated AMC exam conditions

## Technology

- Fine-tuned LLMs for competition math
- Reinforcement learning from human preferences
- Real-time feedback system

## Availability

Available at https://frenzymath.com with free trial for new users.
