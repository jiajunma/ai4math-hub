## About

The Cantor Attic is a comprehensive database of large cardinal axioms in set theory. It catalogs the hierarchy of large cardinals and their interrelationships.

## Content

- **Large cardinal axioms**: From inaccessible cardinals to rank-into-rank
- **Consistency strength**: Implications and equiconsistencies
- **History**: Development of large cardinal theory
- **Open problems**: Current research questions

## Significance

Essential reference for set theorists and researchers in mathematical foundations.
