## Abstract

We introduce AlphaGeometry, an AI system that solves complex geometry problems at the level of International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) gold medalists. Unlike previous approaches, AlphaGeometry does not require human demonstrations for training.

## Key Innovations

- **Synthetic Data**: Generated 100M+ synthetic geometry problems
- **Symbolic Engine**: Deductive reasoning engine
- **LLM**: Neural language model for auxiliary construction
- **Hybrid**: Combines neural and symbolic methods

## Results

- Solved 25/30 IMO geometry problems (previous SOTA: 10/30)
- Near human gold medalist performance

## Significance

Major breakthrough in AI mathematical reasoning published in Nature.
