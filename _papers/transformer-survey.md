## Abstract

We survey the recent advances in applying transformer-based language models to automated theorem proving. We cover model architectures, training strategies, and evaluation benchmarks.

## Coverage

- **GPT-f**: First large-scale application
- **HTPS**: Online training approach
- **Expert Iteration**: Self-improvement
- **LeanDojo**: Retrieval augmentation
- **Benchmarks**: miniF2F, ProofNet, etc.

## Trends

- Increasing model sizes
- Better integration with proof assistants
- More sophisticated search algorithms
- Growing datasets

## Future Directions

- Multimodal reasoning (diagrams + text)
- Better integration with symbolic methods
- Autoformalization pipelines
