## Abstract

Aya is an open-source multilingual language model covering 101 languages. It includes mathematical reasoning capabilities and is fully open for research.

## Mathematical Capabilities

- Reasoning in multiple languages
- STEM question answering
- Competitive performance on math benchmarks

## Open Science

- Model weights released
- Training data documented
- Training code available
- Multilingual focus

## Significance

Represents commitment to open AI research and democratization of AI capabilities worldwide.
