## Abstract

We present AlphaProof, the first AI system to solve mass olympiad-level mathematical problems. It combines pretrained language models with symbolic search algorithms.

## Key Results

- **IMO 2024**: Solved 4 out of 6 problems
- **Silver medal**: Equivalent performance level
- **General approach**: Works across domains

## Method

- **Neural**: Fine-tuned Gemini for formal mathematics
- **Symbolic**: Proof search using Lean
- **Reinforcement learning**: Self-improvement through training

## Significance

First AI system to reach silver medal level at IMO, demonstrating that general-purpose AI can achieve expert-level mathematical reasoning.

## Comparison

- AlphaGeometry: Geometry only
- AlphaProof: General mathematics
- Both use neuro-symbolic approach
