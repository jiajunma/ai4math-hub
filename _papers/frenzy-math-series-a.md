## Funding Announcement

Frenzy Math announced $12 million in Series A funding led by Andreessen Horowitz, with participation from Elad Gil and other notable investors.

## Use of Funds

- **Product Development**: Enhanced AI tutoring capabilities
- **Content Expansion**: Beyond competition math to undergraduate level
- **Global Expansion**: Multi-language support and international curricula
- **Team Growth**: Hiring AI researchers and educators

## Company Growth

- **Users**: 100K+ active students
- **Countries**: 30+ countries represented
- **Problems Solved**: 5M+ math problems completed

## Quote from CEO

"We're building the future of mathematics education. Every student deserves access to world-class math tutoring, and AI makes this possible at scale."

## About Frenzy Math

Founded in 2024, Frenzy Math develops AI-powered tools for mathematics learning, with focus on competition mathematics and problem-solving skills.
