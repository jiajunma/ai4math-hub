## About

Mistral AI is a French artificial intelligence company developing large language models. Known for high-performance open-weight models.

## Models

- **Mistral Large**: Flagship reasoning model
- **Mixtral 8x22B**: Mixture of Experts architecture
- **Codestral**: Code generation specialist
- **Mathstral**: Math-focused model

## Open Models

Mistral has released several open-weight models:
- Mistral 7B
- Mixtral 8x7B
- Mixtral 8x22B

## Performance

Strong results on mathematical benchmarks:
- MATH dataset
- GSM8K
- Competition mathematics

## Le Chat

Conversational AI platform for interacting with Mistral models.

## European Focus

Headquartered in Paris with European data sovereignty focus.
