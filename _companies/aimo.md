## Mission

Create an AI system capable of winning a gold medal at the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO).

## The IMO Grand Challenge

The ultimate goal is to develop AI that can:
- Solve IMO-level geometry, algebra, number theory, and combinatorics problems
- Provide rigorous proofs
- Compete at the level of human gold medalists

## Progress

- **2024**: Multiple AI systems achieve bronze/silver level
- **AlphaGeometry**: Solved geometry problems at gold level
- **Timeline**: Gold medal expected within 5 years

## NuminaMath Competition

AI-MO runs the NuminaMath competition:
- Open challenge for mathematical AI
- Public leaderboard
- Research collaborations

Collaboration between:
