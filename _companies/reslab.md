## About

ResLab is a research laboratory dedicated to advancing AI capabilities in mathematical reasoning and formal methods. The lab brings together researchers from mathematics, computer science, and artificial intelligence.

## Research Areas

- **AI for Mathematics**: Neural theorem proving and mathematical reasoning
- **Formal Verification**: Automated verification of mathematical proofs and software
- **Automated Reasoning**: SAT/SMT solvers and constraint programming
- **Math Education AI**: Intelligent tutoring systems for mathematics

## Projects

- Neural theorem prover development
- Autoformalization of mathematical texts
- Educational AI tools for competition mathematics

## Collaborations

- Academic partnerships with mathematics departments
- Industry collaborations on verification tools
- Open-source contributions to proof assistants

## Team

Researchers from mathematics, computer science, and AI backgrounds working on interdisciplinary projects.
