## About

PRISM AI develops AI tools specifically designed for working mathematicians. Their platform integrates with existing mathematical workflows.

## Products

- **Research Assistant**: Literature analysis and theorem discovery
- **Proof Explorer**: Interactive proof development with AI suggestions
- **Collaboration Tools**: Multi-user mathematical workspaces

## Technology

- Fine-tuned LLMs for mathematical reasoning
- Integration with LaTeX and proof assistants
- Knowledge graphs of mathematical concepts
