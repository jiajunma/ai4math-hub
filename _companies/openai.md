## About

OpenAI is an AI research and deployment company. Their math/reasoning team has produced significant work in neural theorem proving, including GPT-f and HTPS.

## Key Research

- **GPT-f**: First large-scale neural theorem prover
- **HTPS**: HyperTree Proof Search
- **Expert Iteration**: Self-improving theorem provers
- **miniF2F**: Competition math benchmark

## Publications
