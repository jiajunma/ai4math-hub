## About

"我的 Lean 学习笔记" (My Lean Learning Notes) is a Chinese-language resource for learning Lean 4 and formal mathematics. Created by hewzhew, it documents the journey of learning theorem proving.

## Content

- **Lean 4 Basics**: Installation, setup, and basic tactics
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides for beginners
- **Examples**: Worked examples of formal proofs
- **Notes**: Personal learning notes and insights
- **FAQ**: Common questions and solutions

## Language

Content is primarily in **Chinese (中文)**, making it accessible to Chinese-speaking learners interested in formal mathematics.

## Topics Covered

- Natural numbers and basic arithmetic
- Logic and proof techniques
- Functions and sets
- Algebraic structures
- Real analysis basics

## Value

Provides a learner's perspective on formal mathematics, complementing official documentation with personal insights and common pitfalls.

## Community

Open source notes that can help other Chinese speakers learn Lean 4 and formal mathematics.
