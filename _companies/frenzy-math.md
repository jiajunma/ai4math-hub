## About

Frenzy Math is building an AI tutor specifically designed for competitive mathematics and olympiad-level problem solving. Their platform leverages large language models to provide step-by-step guidance through complex mathematical problems.

## Products

- **AI Math Tutor**: Personalized tutoring for competition math
- **Problem Database**: Curated collection of olympiad problems
- **Progress Tracking**: Analytics on student performance

## Target Market

- Math olympiad participants
- High school students preparing for competitions
- Self-learners in advanced mathematics
