## About

Maritza AI is developing next-generation AI systems for mathematical discovery. Their goal is to create AI that can assist mathematicians in formulating conjectures and finding proofs.

## Products

- **Mathematical Discovery Platform**: AI-assisted conjecture generation
- **Proof Assistant**: Natural language interface to theorem provers
- **Mathematical Search**: Semantic search over mathematical literature

## Technology

- Large language models fine-tuned for mathematics
- Integration with formal proof assistants
- Knowledge graphs of mathematical concepts

## Team

Founded by researchers from top AI labs and mathematics departments.
