## About

Harmonic AI develops AI systems that can reason about and solve complex mathematical problems. Their approach combines advances in deep learning with formal mathematical methods.

## Technology

- **Neural Theorem Provers**: AI systems that can prove mathematical theorems
- **Autoformalization**: Converting natural language mathematics to formal proofs
- **Mathematical Reasoning**: General-purpose reasoning for mathematical problems
