## About

Wolfram Research is one of the oldest and most respected companies in computational mathematics. Founded by physicist Stephen Wolfram in 1987, the company has been at the forefront of symbolic computation for decades.

## Products

- **Mathematica**: Industry-standard symbolic computation system
- **Wolfram Alpha**: Computational knowledge engine
- **Wolfram Language**: High-level programming language for technical computing
- **Wolfram Cloud**: Cloud-based computation platform

## AI Integration

- **LLM integration**: Wolfram Language can interface with LLMs
- **WolframAlpha + ChatGPT**: Partnership with OpenAI
- **AI-assisted computation**: Combining symbolic methods with neural approaches

## Research

Wolfram Research maintains active R&D in:
- Automated theorem proving
- Symbolic-numeric hybrid computation
- Knowledge representation
- Natural language understanding for math

## Impact

Mathematica has been used in:
- Thousands of research papers
- Education at major universities
- Industrial R&D
- Government research

## Stephen Wolfram

Founder and CEO Stephen Wolfram is a prominent figure in computational science, known for:
- **A New Kind of Science**: Book on cellular automata and computational universe
- **Wolfram Physics Project**: Attempt to find fundamental theory of physics
- **Views on AI**: Frequent commentator on AI and computation
