## About

AutoDiscovery is an AI-powered research discovery platform developed by the Allen Institute for AI (AI2). It helps researchers discover novel connections, generate hypotheses, and extract insights from scientific literature.

## Features

- **Literature Discovery**: AI-powered search across scientific papers
- **Hypothesis Generation**: Suggests novel research directions
- **Connection Finding**: Discovers links between seemingly unrelated concepts
- **Mathematical Content**: Includes mathematical and computer science literature

## Technology

- Large language models for semantic understanding
- Knowledge graph construction
- Citation network analysis
- Multi-modal document understanding

## Applications for Mathematics

- Finding connections between mathematical fields
- Discovering applications of abstract concepts
- Identifying promising research directions
- Literature review automation

## About AI2

The Allen Institute for AI was founded by Paul G. Allen in 2014 with the mission of conducting high-impact AI research and engineering in service of the common good.
