## About

ReasLab Alpha is an interactive platform that combines AI capabilities with mathematical reasoning. It provides tools for students and researchers to explore mathematical concepts with AI assistance.

## Features

- **Interactive Proofs**: Step-by-step proof exploration with AI guidance
- **Mathematical Reasoning**: AI-powered problem solving assistance
- **Visualizations**: Interactive diagrams and mathematical visualizations
- **Learning Paths**: Structured curriculum for mathematical topics

## Technology

- Large language models for mathematical reasoning
- Interactive web interface
- Real-time collaboration features

## Target Audience

- Mathematics students
- Researchers exploring new concepts
- Self-learners in advanced mathematics

## Platform

Web-based platform accessible at alpha.reaslab.io
