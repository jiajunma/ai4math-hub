## About

Maplesoft is a Canadian software company specializing in mathematical software. Their flagship product, Maple, is used worldwide in education, research, and industry.

## Products

- **Maple**: Symbolic and numeric computing system
- **Maple Learn**: Online math learning environment
- **MapleMBSE**: Model-based systems engineering
- **Maple Flow**: Engineering calculation tool

## Education Focus

Maplesoft has a strong focus on STEM education:
- **Clickable Math**: Interactive problem solving
- **Smart Popups**: Context-sensitive math hints
- **Math Apps**: Interactive educational applications
- **Assessment Tools**: Automated grading of mathematical work

## AI Initiatives

- **AI-powered tutoring**: Development of intelligent tutoring systems
- **Automatic grading**: AI for assessing student work
- **Step-by-step solutions**: AI-generated explanations
