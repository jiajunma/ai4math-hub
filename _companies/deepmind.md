## About

DeepMind is a leading AI research lab. Their mathematical reasoning team has produced breakthrough results including AlphaGeometry and AlphaTensor.

## Key Research

- **AlphaGeometry**: Solves IMO geometry problems
- **AlphaTensor**: Discovers faster matrix multiplication algorithms
- **FunSearch**: LLM-based program search for mathematics
- **AlphaProof**: Neural theorem proving
