## About

Project Numina is an open-source research initiative focused on advancing AI capabilities in mathematical reasoning. The project creates open datasets, trains open models, and establishes benchmarks for the community.

## Key Contributions

- **Datasets**: Large-scale mathematical reasoning datasets
- **Models**: Open-source neural theorem proving models
- **Benchmarks**: Evaluation frameworks for math AI systems
- **Leaderboard**: Public rankings of AI systems on math tasks

## Research Areas

- Neural theorem proving
- Autoformalization
- Mathematical reasoning benchmarks
- Competition math problem solving

## Community

Active open-source community with contributions from researchers worldwide.
