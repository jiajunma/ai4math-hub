## About

Compfiles is a catalog of mathematical problems that have been formalized in Lean. It serves as a resource for finding formalized versions of competition math problems and other mathematical challenges.

## Content

- Competition math problems (AMC, AIME, IMO, etc.)
- Undergraduate mathematics problems
- Research-level formalizations

## Purpose

- Track formalization progress in the Lean community
- Provide examples for learning formal mathematics
- Benchmark for automated theorem provers
