## About

HOList is an environment for neural theorem proving developed by Google Research. It is based on HOL Light and provides infrastructure for training and evaluating neural theorem provers.

## Features

- **HOL Light integration**: Complete theorem prover
- **Neural API**: Interface for ML models
- **Benchmark suite**: Standard problems for evaluation
- **Training data**: Large corpus of HOL proofs

## Research

Used in multiple papers from Google Research:
- DeepHOL
- GNNs for premise selection
- Reinforcement learning for proving

## Datasets

- 29,000+ theorems
- 2,000,000+ proof steps
- Train/validation/test splits

## Significance

One of the first large-scale environments for neural theorem proving research.
