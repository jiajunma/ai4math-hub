## About

mathlib4 is the comprehensive mathematics library for Lean 4. It contains formalized definitions and theorems covering undergraduate and graduate-level mathematics.

## Coverage

- **Algebra**: Groups, rings, fields, linear algebra
- **Analysis**: Calculus, real analysis, complex analysis
- **Topology**: General topology, algebraic topology
- **Number Theory**: Elementary and analytic number theory
- **Measure Theory**: Probability, integration
- **Combinatorics**: Graph theory, enumerative combinatorics

- 100K+ theorems

Essential library for anyone doing mathematics in Lean 4.
