## About

Implementation of Demonstration-Search Planning (DSP), a method that combines language models with search algorithms for automated theorem proving.

## Approach

- **Demonstrations**: Use successful proofs as guidance
- **Search**: Systematic exploration of proof space
- **Planning**: Strategic proof step selection

## Results

Strong performance on miniF2F benchmark.

## Relevance

Influential approach combining neural and symbolic methods.
