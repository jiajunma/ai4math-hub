## About

GeoCoq is a comprehensive formalization of geometry in the Coq proof assistant. It is based on Tarski's axioms and covers both Euclidean and non-Euclidean geometry.

## Content

- **Euclidean geometry**: Book I of Elements formalized
- **Foundations**: Tarski's axioms and their properties
- **Non-Euclidean**: Hyperbolic geometry
- **Arithmetization**: Connection to real numbers

## Significance

One of the most complete formalizations of geometry, covering:
- ~200 theorems
- Multiple axiom systems
- Rigorous proofs of classical results

## Applications

- Education in formal methods
- Verification of geometric algorithms
- Research in automated geometry reasoning

Several papers published about the formalization methodology.
