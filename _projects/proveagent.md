## About

ProveAgent is an experimental project exploring agent-based approaches to automated theorem proving. It investigates how autonomous agents can navigate the proof search space.

## Research Direction

- Agent-based proof search
- Reinforcement learning for theorem proving
- Integration with LLMs
- Multi-agent proof strategies
