## About

ProofNet is a benchmark for autoformalization and neural theorem proving. It contains a parallel corpus of informal and formal mathematical statements.

## Content

- **371 problems** from undergraduate math textbooks
- Parallel informal-formal pairs
- Covers: algebra, analysis, number theory

## Use Cases

- Training autoformalization models
- Evaluating translation quality
- Neural theorem proving benchmark
